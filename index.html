Crafting Dreams Since 2009

Nestled under the open night sky, Moonlight Bakery & Café was born from a simple dream — to bake bread that warms hearts and fills homes with the most comforting aromas imaginable.

For over 15 years, our master bakers have been waking before sunrise, hand-crafting every loaf, pastry, and cake using locally sourced ingredients and generations-old recipes passed down with love.

From our signature matcha bread to our moonlit espresso, every item on our menu is a labor of love and a celebration of the night sky that inspires us.
